Electronics that are nearing the end of their "useful life" are often referred to as "e-waste."E-wastes are regarded as hazardous because some electronic product components include elements that, depending on their density and condition, might be toxic.These materials' toxic composition endangers both human health and the environment. If discarded improperly, old electronics such as computers, televisions, VCRs, stereos, copiers, fax machines, electric lights, cell phones, audio equipment, and batteries can release lead and other materials into the earth and soil.
Many of these products can be

Reused,Reduced and Recycled
in an environmentally sound manner so that they are less harmful to the ecosystem.
This WEBSITE highlights the hazards of e-wastes, the need for its appropriate management and options that can be implemented.
Also website gives the new way to communicate with E-waste producer.

Our goal is to recycle your electronics in a way that is both safe and good for the environment.
